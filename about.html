You know that satisfying popping sound when a can gets opened? Summer Spritiz will bring that satisfaction again with the first sip.
Started as an art project between collaborators Kalissa Tan, Jonathan Chan, Tre Venable, Chris Huynh, Clara Bennett, Hassam Virk, Julia Williams, Matthew Christian, and Caree McDonald, Summer Spiritz was born. Wanting to establish a tasty summer seltzer, our team came together and created Summer Spritiz to raise spirits with tantalizing taste.
